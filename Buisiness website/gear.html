John Abraham

Guitars

Schecter Damine Elite

John primarily uses his Schecter Damien Elite 7-string, which has active EMG 81 and 85
(bridge and nexk respectively), and a floyd rose special tremolo bridge.

Amplifiers

Peavey Triple XXX Head

The Triple X is the main amplifier used for the band. This 3 channel amp boasts 3 12ax7 preamp tubes
and 4 kt77 poweramp tubes.
